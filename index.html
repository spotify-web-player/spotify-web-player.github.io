    Why can't I log into Spotify web player?

    Here's how you can resolve issues you might face occasionally while trying to login your Spotify account:

    • Restart Browser: Reloading the tabs again might resolve the issue. When too many tabs are opened, it may hamper working of Spotify Web Player.
    • Login Again: Fix the issue by logging into your account again after logging out of it.
    • Sign Out your Account from every device: Signing out of multiple devices might work to resolve "Spotify web player Not Working Issue". Simply, tap on the Profile Icon on the Top right Corner. Thereafter, go to Accounts. Now, select the 'Account Overview' section. Then, tap on “Sign out everywhere” option. Now, you'll be signed out of every device at once successfully.
    • Activate Protected Content On Chrome: Enable the 'Protected Content On Chrome' option. To do so, click on the three dots at top-right corner. Thereafter, select 'Settings' option, Tap on the 'Privacy and Security'. From here, tap on the 'Site Settings' then 'Additonal Content Settings' option. On the 'Protected content IDs' drop-down, tap on “Sites can play protected content”. Referesh browser and login Spotify web again.
    • Clear Cache: Clearing the Browser Cache might work to load the Spotify Web Player content easily. You can clear Cache by :
      1. Head to Settings Panel, then selecting 'Privacy & Security'.
      2. Here, click on 'Clear browsing data'.
      3. Under time range drop-down menu, click on 'All time'.
      4. Clear browser cache by tapping 'Clear data'.
    • Check Internet Connection: You might face trouble if Internet Connection is Poor. So ensure that a stable connection is connected.
    • Unable Ad-Blockers: Visit the 'chrome://extensions/' through your web-browser. Now, you'll see Extensions you've installed. Hover over the On/Off button to make changes to settings of these Extensions.

    How to Enable Dark Mode in Spotify web player?

    Lets see how to activate dark mode in Spotify Web Player-

    1. Open web browser and head on to 'Spotify Web Player'.
    2. Then, login your account.
    3. Now, tap on the Profile icon.
    4. From drop-down menu, select "Settings".
    5. Thereafter, tap on the 'Display options'.
    6. Now, turn On 'Dark Mode'.
